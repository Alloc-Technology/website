faqs:
  - title: What industries do you specialize in?
    answer: We work with companies of all sizes across technology, finance, healthcare, and e-commerce. Our expertise spans from startups building their initial infrastructure to enterprises modernizing legacy systems.

  - title: What cloud platforms do you support?
    answer: We are platform-agnostic and work with all major cloud providers including AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, and hybrid/multi-cloud environments. We help you select the best platform based on your specific requirements.

  - title: How do you ensure security in the delivery pipeline?
    answer: We implement DevSecOps practices including automated security scanning, container vulnerability assessments, secrets management, and compliance monitoring. Security is integrated at every stage rather than treated as an afterthought.

  - title: Can you work with our existing team?
    answer: Absolutely. We can either lead your infrastructure initiatives or work alongside your existing engineering team. We provide knowledge transfer and documentation to ensure long-term maintainability.

  - title: What is your typical engagement model?
    answer: We offer flexible engagement models including project-based implementations, ongoing managed services, and team augmentation. We start with an assessment to understand your needs and recommend the best approach.

  - title: How quickly can you onboard and start delivering value?
    answer: Most engagements can begin within 1-2 weeks. We prioritize quick wins and iterative delivery so you can see measurable improvements in your development velocity and infrastructure reliability early in the partnership.

  - title: Do you provide training and certification support?
    answer: Yes, we offer training programs for teams looking to build internal DevOps capabilities. This includes workshops on Kubernetes, Terraform, CI/CD best practices, and cloud architecture.
